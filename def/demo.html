The Sign Language Detector uses advanced computer vision technology to recognize hand gestures in real-time. Here's how it works:

  1. Camera Access: The app accesses your device's camera
  2. Hand Detection: MediaPipe identifies hand landmarks in each frame
  3. Gesture Analysis: Custom algorithms analyze finger positions
  4. Text Conversion: Detected gestures are converted to words
  5. Real-time Display: Results are shown instantly on screen
